title: "Quick Tip: Better Prompts for AI Agents"
date: 2025-01-20
tags: [AI agent, prompt engineering, tips]
excerpt: "A simple technique to get better results from AI agents: be specific about the output format you want."
---

When working with AI agents, specificity is key. Instead of asking "explain this code," try:

"Explain this code in three parts: 1) What it does, 2) How it works, 3) Potential improvements."

This structured approach gives the AI a clear framework and typically results in more useful, organized responses.
